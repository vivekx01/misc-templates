Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

  • Q: What are your working hours?

    A: We are open from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

  • Q: How can I book an appointment?

    A: You can book an appointment by calling our helpline at +1 (234) 567-89 or by using our online booking system.

  • Q: What insurance plans do you accept?

    A: We accept a wide range of insurance plans. Please bring your insurance card and valid identification for verification.

Billing and Payments

  • Q: How can I pay my medical bills?

    A: We accept various payment methods, including cash, credit cards, and online payment platforms. You can pay your bills at our billing counter or through our online payment portal.

  • Q: Can I set up a payment plan for my medical expenses?

    A: Yes, we offer payment plans for eligible patients. Please contact our billing department to discuss your options.
